Benefits of procuring steel online

The steel industry has been offline for decades. Information regarding delivery and inventory is scarce or often, incomplete. 

The purchase manager’s list of steel supplier and distributors is limited. When undertaking a project, the executive has to source suppliers and involve in tedious, long drawn conversations with them. The choices are limited, processes are piling up and costs seem to increase everyday. 

Steel procurement then becomes a costly affair especially  when many companies are looking to cut costs. 

 By digitizing procurement, customers will be privy to a transparent, easy to access page which reduces the overdrawn processes of sourcing the right supplier for their business needs. 

 

Buying Steel online

When customers procure steel from online steel suppliers, they will be able to experience a transparent purchase system which provides quick, timely updates regarding their transactions. 

Steel distributors in the USA who are looking to digitize by connecting with online marketplaces stand to gain big as the industry incorporates technology in its procurement processes. 

The consultancy firm PwC describes digitalisation as one of important competencies of steel manufacturers in the upcoming years. The United States online steel marketplace has begun its journey to catch up to the giant Chinese players like Ouyeel and Zhaogang, as intense competition and overcapacity is spurring start ups to come up with innovative business models.
